Swedish International Center

Swedish International Center for Dialogue and Research and Civilizations and Comparative Religions Study

Strategy of the Center

1. Spreading of dialogue culture and acceptance of the other and peaceful coexistence among religions followers
2. Dialogue of civilizations and cultures
3. Clarification of the mission and philosophy of Islam ,peace and tolerance

The Vision

- The center shall be a leading example for achievement of peaceful co-existence between the different religions followers and international reference for enrichment of dialogue.
- The center will be a model for leadership at the local, regional and international effort to prudent contribution in transit towards a bright future.

The Mission :-
The center seeks constructive dialogue between the followers of religions ,civilizations and cultures in order to best understanding of the religious principles and teachings so as to utilize them for serving the whole humanity ,starting with mutual dialogue and recognition of difference and enrichment of scientific activity with research ,authoring and execution of specialty programs and activities and widening of the horizons of cultural closeness ,fostering competitiveness with researchers and similar institutions, beginning with the center work fields and complying with scientific and academic standards investing the technical and human resources in an effective partnership frame for opening the dialogue horizons and internal and external communication.

Programs of the Center

A- Annual conference:

The Center holds an annual conference for Religions, Civilizations and cultures Dialogue, in which conference some of the important humanitarian issues are addressed from the perspective of every religion to reach a common outcome that serves the values of justice and peace.

Spreading the culture of dialogue and peaceful coexistence and acceptance of others

B- Seminars:

These are seminars and workshops and round table specialized to discuss some of the topics of common interest between the followers of different religions, civilizations and cultures whereas to them are called specialists, religion scholars and researchers to enrich these topics. The results of such discussions shall be published and issued in the brochures by the Center periodically.

C- Scientific Journal:

The Center will publish a quarterly magazine in Swedish, English and Arabic languages and shall be of a scientific and systematic cultural including many of the perfect researches and discusses various issues of intellectual, humanitarian and dialogue between religions, civilizations and cultures.

D- Researches and Studies:

The Centre Shall furnish the researches and studies in various fields such as commonalities between religions and field studies of the priority issues and analysis of the Center’s performance indicators.

E- Translation services:

The center offers translation services for articles, research and specialized studies and everything related to the field of its message, which calls for dialogue between religions, civilizations and cultures as well as the interpretation of the seminars organized by the Center and others.
